Goldbach Fail Anticipation Engine

Hunts where a Goldbach failure would hide. Computes a riskScore for every even E, zooms into the top-20 danger rows with prime-blocker labels, and ranks the weakest witnesses. This is the right kind of paranoia — not celebrating passes, finding where failure lives.
riskScore = pressure × 50 + (1/S) × 100 + (1/ĉ) × 10  |  S = witness pair count  |  ĉ = S / (E / log²E) density model  |  pressure = blocked offsets / total offsets before first survivor
