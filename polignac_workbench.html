Polignac Conjecture Engine

For every positive even integer k, there are infinitely many consecutive prime pairs (p, p+k). This engine computes finite certificates for all tested even gaps k, finds the weakest gap, measures density constants, and documents exactly where the universal proof remains open.

This engine provides proof-pressure evidence and finite certificates for Polignac's Conjecture. It is not a proof that prime gaps of every even size occur infinitely often. PASS means no obstruction was found inside the tested range. PASS does not mean proof. The universal theorem remains open for all even k.
